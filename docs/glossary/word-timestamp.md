# word-timestamp

A **word timestamp** is a word with `start` and `end` times (seconds) used for caption timing and word highlighting.

## Where it appears

- `timestamps.json`: `TimestampsOutputSchema.allWords[]`
- `timestamps.json`: `SceneTimestampSchema.words[]` (when scene grouping is present)
