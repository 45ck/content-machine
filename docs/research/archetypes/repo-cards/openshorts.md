# Repo Card: mutonby/openshorts

## Why It Matters

It is the broadest productized reference: clip generator, AI UGC shorts,
thumbnail/title tooling, galleries, and social publishing.

## How It Makes Shorts

For UGC/product shorts:

1. Scrape or research product.
2. Extract pain points, praise, competitors, differentiators, and viral angles.
3. Generate hook/problem/solution/CTA scripts.
4. Generate or select actor/avatar image.
5. Generate voiceover.
6. Generate talking-head/lip-sync video.
7. Generate B-roll.
8. Compose with subtitles and hook overlay.

For clips:

1. Transcribe source media.
2. Detect viral moments.
3. Reframe to 9:16.
4. Add captions and hooks.
