# Experiment Brief

Produce a vertical short-form explainer that relies on stock B-roll
instead of gameplay or talking-head footage.

Constraints:

- Keep all generated artifacts under this lane folder.
- Prefer the repo's built-in skills and harness scripts.
- Aim for one real, reviewable MP4 rather than a mock placeholder.
- If the environment prevents a real run, capture the exact blocker and
  the exact files that were still produced.

Proposed topic:

`How password managers actually protect you`

Reasoning:

- Easy to illustrate with generic stock footage
- Concrete enough for an explainer archetype
- Does not depend on niche visuals or local media
