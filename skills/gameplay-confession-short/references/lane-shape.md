# Gameplay Confession Lane Shape

Use this for the generic storytime/confession lane when a Reddit post
card is not the right opener.

Pattern name: `gameplay-confession-split`.

Default variant: `subject-safe-split`.

Fill variants:

- `crop-fill-split` when the important subject survives the crop.
- `contained-blur-split` when crop-fill makes the important subject too
  soft or cuts it off.

## Shape

1. Open with the stakes in the first line.
2. Optional `2s` to `4s` opener card if there is a quote, receipt, or
   accusation worth isolating.
3. Run story-related motion or receipt crops on the top half.
4. Keep continuous gameplay on the bottom half.
5. Overlay captions at the midpoint with active-word emphasis.
6. End on the judgment, reveal, or comment-bait turn.

## Lane Fill Defaults

- Start by removing black gutters, not by blindly scaling.
- If the source clip has black side gutters and the subject survives,
  crop them out before scaling.
- If crop-fill hurts the shot, use a blurred duplicate of the same clip
  as the lane background and place the contained source over it.
- Never preserve plain black gutters as the final default.

## What Works

- roommate drama
- cheating/confession stories
- job/interview horror stories
- family or wedding conflict stories
- anonymous “I did this and it blew up” storytimes

## What To Avoid

- long static screenshots as the entire top lane
- top-lane shots lasting `8s+`
- gameplay as the only changing visual
- giant opener cards that push the real story start too late
