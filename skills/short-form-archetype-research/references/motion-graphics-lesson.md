# Recipe: Motion Graphics Lesson

## Inputs

- Concept or lesson brief.
- Timed beats or script.
- Style/motion grammar.

## Build Steps

1. Split lesson into 3-6 beats.
2. Assign each beat a scene type and motion purpose.
3. Generate or select motion/card assets.
4. Render timed scenes.
5. Add voice/caption layer.
6. Generate contact sheet.
7. Review text fit, motion clarity, and payoff.

## Required Reviews

- One idea per beat.
- Text fits on mobile.
- Motion clarifies rather than decorates.
- Final payoff resolves the concept.
