# Repo Card: gyoridavid/short-video-maker

## Why It Matters

It is the closest TypeScript/Remotion faceless explainer reference. It keeps
the runtime pragmatic: Kokoro TTS, Whisper captions, Pexels search, Remotion
composition, REST and MCP surfaces.

## How It Makes Shorts

1. Accept scene inputs: narration text and search terms.
2. Generate TTS audio.
3. Transcribe generated audio for captions.
4. Search Pexels for portrait background video.
5. Pick music by tag/mood.
6. Render portrait or landscape Remotion composition.
