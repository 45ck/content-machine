# Recipe: UGC/avatar Product Short

## Inputs

- Product URL or product brief.
- Audience and offer.
- Claim bank or allowed claims.

## Build Steps

1. Extract product facts, pain points, differentiators, and proof.
2. Build claim bank.
3. Write hook/problem/solution/proof/CTA script.
4. Generate or select avatar with provenance.
5. Generate voiceover.
6. Generate talking-head/lip-sync segment.
7. Generate B-roll/proof shots.
8. Compose hook, captions, face, and proof without overlap.
9. Review claims and synthetic media provenance.

## Required Reviews

- Every product claim has evidence.
- Avatar and voice provenance are recorded.
- Synthetic content policy is known for target platform/account.
- CTA is honest and specific.
