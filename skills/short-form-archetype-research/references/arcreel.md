## Archetype

Story/video workspace; useful for `motion-graphics-lesson`,
`topic-faceless-explainer`, and longer narrative lanes.

## Production Pattern

Novel or story input becomes scripts, character/clue design sheets,
storyboards, generated clips, and final composition through a web workspace
with task queues and asset versioning.

## Useful Extraction

- character and prop continuity artifacts
- version history and rollback
- provider abstraction for image/video/text models
- cost tracking and estimates
- CapCut draft export
- project import/export
- task queue with resume/checkpoint behavior

## Content-Machine Implication

Generated media needs asset versioning and continuity references before it can
scale beyond one-off shorts.
